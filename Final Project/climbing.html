Climbing

I became interested in climbing through watching films like 180 South and To the Limit. This interest persisted for a couple of years, also including a couple of books on or near the topic. But I didn't actually go climbing anywhere until a conversation with one of my colleagues, who invited me up to Earth Treks Climbing Center in Rockville. I made my debut in December 2014 and have visited Earth Treks only a weekly-plus basis since then.

Climbing appeals to our natural sense of adventure. It combines physical and mental engagement in an unusual way. You can't get up the wall through brute strength, and you can't think your way up either. There are more and less elegant ways to do it, and the more elegant way is often the more effective way -- style is a guide to success.
